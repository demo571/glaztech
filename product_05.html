Econo Master

The well-tested technology of the EconoMaster drive system with 2 wheels and a counter wheel each ensures high numbers of cycles and minimal wear. Due to door leaf weights up to 130 kg, extensive passage widths can be implemented using our slim G30 profile system or using thermally separated profile systems at an exceptional price-performance relationship. Whether your door assembly comprises drive-reliable and simple-to-fit sliding panels only or comprises sidelights and fanlights in addition, G-U Automatic provides you with complete service, including automatic door installation, commissioning, and maintenance. The drives are type-tested according to DIN 18650 by TUV and meet the guidelines and standards prevailing at present. The EM-F drive system is tested and is approved for installation in emergency and escape routes (German regulation AutSchR).

Compact Master

With its neat dimensions and elegant, pleasing looks, the Compact Master inline sliding door blends particularly well with architecture-tender glass and accentuates its generosity and transparency. With a "real" drive height of just 100 mm, the top door panel profile of the slim profile system is incorporated into the drive and covered by a cover. Slim and technically ideal large carriage wheels and a rubber mat below the running track provide excellent running smoothness. The drives are type-tested by TUV according to DIN 18650 and therefore meet the current standards and regulations. The redundant drive system CM-F is also suitable for application in escape and rescue paths (AutSchR, German directive for automatic entrance doors in rescue paths). We also provide professional automatic door installation for these systems.

Heavy Master

The Heavy Master sliding door drive is the solution for your special needs. Sliding panels with large sizes or heavy weights up to 200 kg are driven quietly and reliably. Even in highly loaded entrance points with very heavy traffic, the Heavy Master achieves perfect running behavior with minimum wear, due to the stable trolley and rubber-coated running wheel. Sturdy and efficient sliding panels from steel or featuring thermally decoupled system profiles can be ideally combined with the Heavy Master. The drives are certified to DIN 18650 and type-tested according to TUV and meet today's guidelines and standards. HM-F drive system is tested and approved for mounting in emergency and escape routes (German regulation AutSchR). As top suppliers of automatic doors in Dubai, we ensure our automatic entrance doors are of top quality.

Standard Revolving door

Standard revolving doors make sure building entrances work, exclude draughts, and are soundproof. The flexible design and variety of options enable individual solutions with differing function, appearance, and size. The classic revolving door can be opened manually, semi-automatically by triggering the Push & Go function, or automatically by triggering the radar sensors, as needed. The drum walls can be equipped with glass but also with heat-insulated sheet metal panels. No matter what option is selected, user safety is always the priority. The semi-automatic and automatic revolving door are type-tested by TÜV to DIN 18650 and EN 16005 and meet the current standards and regulations. We also have expertise in automatic door installation for different types of revolving doors

a. Security curved sliding door

The entire security package: The security curved sliding door is ideal for places like banks, where access control systems are fitted in spaces where security is of utmost importance. Entry into these spaces should be thoroughly regulated. Only a single individual is allowed access to the middle space at a time. When the individual enters the circular compartment, one of the doors shuts. The person will only be able to pass through the second leaf once they have been identified as an authorized individual. The doors feature a large amount of glass for easy identification. The roof of the circular compartment is waterproof, making it suitable for outdoor use as well. For secure and reliable installations, choose automatic door installation by professionals.

b. Large Capacity Revolving Door

For high flow rates of visitors and application on escape and rescue routes, the Large Capacity Revolving Door is the stylish and energy-efficient door solution. With a contemporary design, it is extremely functional in its best possible combination. Ideal for escape and rescue routes, leaves are split in two and supported safely with magnetic clamps under normal conditions. In case of fire, power failure, or if the emergency button has been activated, the panel fixation is released and the sliding panels fold away at the center pillar in the direction of escape. The fully automatic revolving door is fixed with sensors and contact strips in accordance with the standard DIN 18650 and type-tested by TUV. We are professional automatic doors suppliers in Dubai that provide automatic entrance doors to address such high-capacity requirements.

c. All Glass Revolving Door

All Glass Revolving Doors are not only functional but also highly presentable. The minimum profile sizes allow maximum transparency, even in automatically revolving glass doors, because the drive is placed in the floor. Sophisticated and welcoming customer-specific solutions, like swing-out leaves for summer opening or automatic night lock function, can always be added and installed at short notice. The semi-automatic and automatic revolving doors are type-tested by TUV and certified to DIN 18650 and meet the current regulations and standards. For these premium systems, we provide professional automatic door installation services.

d. Security Revolving Door

Safety-sensitive buildings or building areas can be controlled through security revolving doors. In addition to functionality, the building architecture is also given attention based on the desires of the customer, so there is always a custom solution. The security revolving door is motor-powered and released by an access system. Unauthorized individuals are guided out of the drum in the direction of exit.

Automatic Swing Door

Automatic closing is desirable with fire and smoke protection doors. However, a closed door always represents an obstruction. Nevertheless, convenience and accessibility can only be ensured if the door is easy to open. Automatic swing-door drives from the GU Group ensure secure closure yet allow convenient access via the radar motion sensor, pushbutton or access control system. Entrance doors in the facade can also make use of the benefits of the swing-door drive: when combined with multi-point locking, burglar protection and escape route safety are equally possible as well as ensuring accessibility
